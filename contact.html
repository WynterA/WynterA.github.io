DraftVue is an iOS Fantasy Football Draftboard Visualizer and Manager. \n Available for iPhone and iPads running iOS 12 or later!


Features include:
  • Sync with your CBS Fantasy Football league's draft
  • Visualize on your app and or apple tv devices
  • Start your own draft manually
  • No sticky notes or poster board needed!
